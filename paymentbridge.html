What's PaymentBridge?
PaymentBridge is a tool that allows Bitcoin Cash and Bitcoin users who don't have a Payment Protocol compatible wallet to pay BitPay invoices. You just have to paste the Payment Protocol link and use your wallet as you would do normally. After this tool shows you the address and the amount, a transaction should be made and the TXID should be pasted in this tool, to complete the purchase. The payment can be cancelled in any step. It's safe to use this tool, in the worst case, you'll likely get a refund from BitPay.
This tool is no way made in affiliation with or endorsed by BitPay.
Everything is done on your device, except the use of BitPay's servers and using Bitcoin.com explorer to acquire the raw transaction.
